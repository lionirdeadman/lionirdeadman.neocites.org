Mistakes

I'm still uncertain about doing this but I have a certain belief that I need to expose my mistakes, expose the cracks that make me -- well, me. Those terrible experiences that I wish I had no need to talk about, that I wish I could just hide and cast away far far away. But I think that's wrong, hiding them doesn't make me better, it just avoids the social consequences of my mistakes.

Yes, I need to be accountable and learn from them. Fleeing can only result in denial and I would learn nothing from being in denial, I could only hate myself rather than accepting of my mistakes.
